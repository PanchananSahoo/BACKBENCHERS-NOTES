Training and Placement Cell

The Panchanan Sahoo Training & Placement Cell is equipped with all the resources necessary to help worthy students find competitive employment. By staying in touch with reputable companies and industrial facilities, the Training & Placement Cell plays a significant role in discovering work prospects for Undergraduates and Postgraduates graduating from the college. To foster connections between employers and graduates, the Placement Cell is open all year round. The number of students hired after campus interviews keeps increasing. Numerous reputable industries conduct interviews at the institute after being invited.
This Department prepares the students for the process of recruitment and simultaneously create awareness among companies about the recruitment opportunities at Panchanan Sahoo.

The competition for jobs is currently getting tougher every day, making placement a difficult challenge. The institution now has a significant obligation to educate pupils and give them life skills. In addition to technical proficiency, holistic personality development is also required.
A full-fledged training cell is operating in our college to improve the capabilities of engineering graduates to be on par with industry standards in order to meet these objectives.
